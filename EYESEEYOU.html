Next to the paper is an assortment of letters that you can feel and arrange like letter blocks, it’s just so you don’t forget the translation. You notice there’s a chain tethered to the wall under the table that seems to have a 4 number combination lock. On the table there is an assortment of four letters, these for letters are 1, 2, 7, & 8. Next to it is a paper that says “These numbers have gone used, chained to society, set them free.” If you figure it out and assort them in the right way by inputting 7128 to the lock, you will see a green light flash on and off in the bottom corner of the floor and in that corner, a dirty old box will be revealed, and inside of the box is a picture with 4 people in it, faces obscured, next to it is a note that reads “Together Forever - W.D.” in wingdings, once you open the box, the lights turn off except for the green light, this music starts playing, and you can take off the blindfold to look at the picture for until the music stops. There’s another puzzle in this room, on a bookshelf you can find a container with a broken music box inside that you can fix by tuning it and putting the strings back in their places. Once you fix it and play the box fully (which has this song), a locked refrigerator opens (with a remote by me,) and inside is a single egg, the same music starts playing, the lights turn off, and you can take off the blindfold for until the music stops. There is one final puzzle in this room, and that is the puzzle to open the door. The door has a ledge on it with a note that reads ”When the words of change are spoken, the key to freedom will be unlocked.” In a cabinet nearby, there is a paper that has a bunch of words and letters on it that make out a psychology essay, but inside of it, there is one line that you should pick up on since it’s bolded in the text that reads “When thinking about human nature and its fears, the key is appearance.” If you say appearance out loud, a drawer will unlock with the keys to the door, and you can walk outside.
